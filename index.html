Course Curriculum

Master the art of traditional and Western-style embroidery with comprehensive training in various stitches, patterns, and thread techniques.

  • Traditional and Western-style embroidery patterns
  • Various types of stitches and designs
  • Thread and color combination techniques

Learn professional fabric painting techniques including color blending, shading, and design composition for various applications.

  • Color blending, shading, and design composition
  • Practical work on sarees, kurtis, dupattas, bags, and home décor items

Comprehensive training in garment construction from basic drafting to perfect fitting, including various blouse and outfit designs.

  • 10 types of blouse designs
  • 5 types of designer kurtis

  • Complete training from basic drafting to perfect fitting

Learn to create 10 different types of bags including handbags, totes, sling bags, and clutches with professional finishing techniques.

  • Total 10 types of bags (handbag, tote, sling, clutch, etc.)

Create beautiful home décor items including pillow covers and wall frames using the techniques learned throughout the course.

  • Pillow covers
  • Wall frames and more

Master various brooch design techniques and patchwork methods to create a unique designer collection.

  • Various brooch design techniques
  • Patchwork techniques and creating a creative designer collection
